Tobias Vincent Maguire (born June 27, 1975) is an American actor and film producer. He is best known for starring as Spider-Man in Sam Raimi's Spider-Man trilogy (2002â€“2007), a role he later reprised in Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021).

Maguire started his career in supporting roles in the films This Boy's Life (1993), The Ice Storm, Deconstructing Harry (both 1997), and Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas (1998). His leading roles include Pleasantville (1998), Ride with the Devil (1999), The Cider House Rules (1999), Wonder Boys (2000), Seabiscuit (2003), The Good German (2006), Brothers (2009), The Great Gatsby (2013), and Pawn Sacrifice (2014).

Timeline of Vincent's life

  1. 1975: Born on June 27 in Santa Monica, California.
  2. 1989-1990s: Starts acting in commercials and TV shows like Roseanne and Blossom.
  3. 1997: Gains recognition in The Ice Storm.
  4. 1999: Stars in The Cider House Rules, earning critical acclaim.
  5. 2002: Breakthrough role as Spider-Man in Sam Raimi's Spider-Man.
  6. 2004: Stars in Spider-Man 2, widely praised as a top superhero film.
  7. 2007: Appears in Spider-Man 3, the highest-grossing of the trilogy.
  8. 2009: Earns a Golden Globe nomination for Brothers.
  9. 2012: Plays Nick Carraway in The Great Gatsby.
  10. 2021: Returns as Spider-Man in Spider-Man: No Way Home, reuniting with other Spider-Man actors.
  11. Personal: Marries Jennifer Meyer in 2007 (two children), separates in 2016.
